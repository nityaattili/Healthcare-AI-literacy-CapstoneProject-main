Healthcare AI literacy portal — optional seeded accounts
================================================================

To create these three accounts automatically on an EMPTY database, set:

  export AIL_SEED_STANDARD_ACCOUNTS=1

before starting Streamlit. You can override passwords with:

  AIL_SEED_ADMIN_PASSWORD
  AIL_SEED_INSTRUCTOR_PASSWORD
  AIL_SEED_STUDENT_PASSWORD

Default seeded credentials (change after first login):

  Role          Username      Default password
  ------------  ------------  --------------------------------
  admin         admin         Admin@Healthcare2026!
  instructor    instructor    Instructor@Healthcare2026!
  student       student       Student@Healthcare2026!

If AIL_SEED_STANDARD_ACCOUNTS is not set, the database stays empty until you
complete the first-time administrator form on the sign-in screen.

Administrators assign instructors to students under Permissions & users
(supervisor column). Instructors see all students on their dashboard;
rows assigned to them are listed first.
